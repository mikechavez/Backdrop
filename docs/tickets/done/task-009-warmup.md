# Warm-Up Phase (T-36 to T-24)

## Objective
Prime the social algorithms and your network before launch. The goal is to make your account "active" in people's feeds so that when the launch tweet drops, it gets immediate engagement.

## Context
Cold launches fail. If your last tweet was 3 weeks ago and you suddenly drop a long thread, the algorithm buries it. The warm-up creates engagement momentum.

## Tool Routing
- Tool: Manual
- Model: N/A

## Execution Steps

### Evening before launch day
1. **Post cryptic teaser tweet** (from TASK-008). No links, just intrigue.
2. **Reply thoughtfully to 10 large accounts** in AI/dev Twitter:
   - Find tweets about AI coding, agents, developer tools
   - Write genuine, substantive replies (not "great post!")
   - This puts your profile in front of their audiences
3. **DM 5 builder friends** asking for launch-day RT support:
   - Be specific: "I'm launching tomorrow morning — would you RT my first tweet?"
   - Send the tweet draft so they know what they're amplifying
4. **Line up 3 people to quote-tweet you**:
   - Ask them to QT with their own take within the first hour
   - Early QTs signal to the algorithm that the content is worth distributing
5. **Prepare media assets**:
   - 2 screenshots of the interactive site (graph + routing game)
   - 1 short screen recording (15–30s walkthrough of the simulator)
   - These are for standalone tweets and replies on launch day

### Pre-launch checklist
6. **Schedule calendar block** for launch hour (no meetings, no distractions)
7. **Prepare livestream outline** (15–25 min):
   - Walk through the credential leak story live
   - Demo the interactive simulator
   - Show the actual cost spiral data
   - Take questions

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Teaser tweet posted
- [ ] 10 genuine, substantive replies posted on AI/dev Twitter
- [ ] 5 DMs sent to friends with RT ask + tweet draft
- [ ] 3 QT commitments confirmed
- [ ] 2 screenshots + 1 screen recording ready
- [ ] Calendar blocked for launch
- [ ] Livestream outline written (if doing)

## Dependencies
- All dev work done (TASK-003 — deploy)
- All content ready (TASK-008 — launch copy)
- Interactive site is live and working

## Out of Scope

- Influencer outreach to strangers
